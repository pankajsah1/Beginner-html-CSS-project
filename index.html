Gulab Jamun


Ingredients


  1. Milk
  2. Sugar
  3. Floar
  4. Oil
  5. Water
  6. Milk Powder

Recipe


  • Step 1: Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire.
    Add cardamom and keep aside to cool

  • Step 2: In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji,
    Milk Powder, baking powder and baking soda.
    Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.

  • Step 3: Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and
    gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil
    at very low flame till golden brown in color

  • Step 4: Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup.
    Once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it
    to a boil and remove from flame.

  • Step 5: Serve warm.

Barfi


Ingredients


  1. Milk
  2. Sugar
  3. Powdered milk
  4. Ghee / clarified butter
  5. Cardamom Powder
  6. Dry Fruits

Recipe


  • Step 1: Crush dry fruits to a fine powder. Put all the ingredients together
    and grind to a smooth paste.

  • Step 2: Transfer to a thick bottomed kadai and cook on a slow fire
    until the mixture starts leaving the slides and forms a ball.

  • Step 3: Put the mixture in a tray and roll out thinly (1/8”).
    Cool and cut into diamond/square shapes.

Besan Laddu


Ingredients


  1. Coarse Besan
  2. Refined oil
  3. Ghee
  4. Chopped Dry Fruits
  5. Milk Powder

Recipe


  • Step 1: Take heavy bottom kadai, add ghee, oil and besan.
    Cook on medium flame for 10 to 12 minutes or till besan turns golden brown.
    Add chopped nuts, mix well and keep aside to cool down.

  • Step 2: Once cool, add the Nestlé MILKMAID and mix well,
    keep aside for 15 minutes.

  • Step 3: Divide the mixture into 24 portions and gently roll into laddus.
